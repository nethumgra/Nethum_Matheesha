About Me

Creative Graphic Designer & Visual Storyteller

I'm Nethum Matheesha, a 17-year-old creative graphic designer with over 2 years of hands-on experience. My design journey started when I was 15, fueled by a passion for visual communication and digital art.

I specialize in creating memorable brand identities through thoughtful logo design, cohesive branding systems, and engaging social media graphics. My approach combines artistic sensitivity with strategic thinking to deliver designs that not only look exceptional but also effectively communicate your brand's unique story.
